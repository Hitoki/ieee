About the Project

The IEEE Technology Navigator is a web-based discovery tool that helps IEEE members, prospective members, and corporate partners to connect to IEEE resources.

Unlike standard search engines, Technology Navigator uses visual navigation to present key concepts that match a user's needs and interests. Industry, education and government users can now connect to all IEEE products and services from a single point of entry. This approach allows users to "drill down" from general industry and technology terms to specific IEEE resources - societies & councils, publications, conferences and standards.

Presenting IEEE information through the Technology Navigator:

  • Enhances access to resources relevant to real-world applications
  • Breaks down discipline-specific silos
  • Provides access to information and resources regarding emerging technologies
  • Identifies areas of intersection that will encourage associations to cooperate in overlapping discipline areas.

The development of Technology Navigator began in late 2008 with a fully-functional version completed in March, 20089. This version included an interface to enable IEEE volunteers to collaboratively populate this tool. Version 1 launched in June 2009 and enables users to navigate through industry areas and technology concepts to discover a matching set of societies/councils and core IEEE product resources.

Version 2, to be completed in early 2010, extends the Technology Navigator to the larger IEEE community and to the general public. Version 2 will also integrate and extend the types of data that can be displayed, including selected IEEE XPlore content.

The goals for the Technology Navigator are to help IEEE to:

  • Become a global information resource.
  • Be the preferred place to go for timely, relevant scientific information.
  • Be recognized as the leading organization for forming new knowledge communities, delivering quality information and supporting technical professionals.
  • Connect industry "edge users" to "rich" and relevant IEEE content.
  • Gather together IEEE Societies and Councils, Conferences, Standards and Publications in one place.

Questions or comments about the Technology Navigator? Contact us at technav [at] ieee [dot] org.

About the Team

Allan previously was a founder of three venture-funded startups.
